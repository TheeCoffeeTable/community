Our Programs

Community‑led initiatives supporting wellbeing, connection and empowerment

THEE Coffee Table delivers practical programs that respond to real community needs. Each program is delivered through our local groups and adapted to reflect culture, context and community voice.

Training & Skills Development

Confidence‑building programs designed to support women in developing skills, leadership and pathways toward greater independence.

  • Creative skills – art, craft and sewing
  • Enterprise and small business support
  • Leadership and confidence building
  • Work‑readiness and life skills

Delivered across selected community groups based on local interest.

Community Kitchen & Food Support

Programs addressing food insecurity while strengthening dignity, connection and care within the community.

  • Community Kitchen (Ndooni)
  • Food Bank and food relief initiatives
  • Shared meals for families and seniors
  • Basic food handling and hospitality skills

Delivered where kitchen and storage facilities are available.

Wellbeing & Community Connection

Holistic programs supporting emotional wellbeing, cultural connection and social inclusion across life stages.

  • Seniors wellness and support groups
  • Talanoa on the Mat
  • Youth programs
  • Community connection and wellbeing activities

Programs are shaped by the needs of each local community.
