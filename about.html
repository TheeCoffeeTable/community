About THEE Coffee Table

Our story, our values, and the community we serve

THEE Coffee Table was established on 7 July 2021 during the COVID‑19 pandemic in response to increasing isolation, anxiety, domestic violence and financial hardship impacting multicultural women.

What began as three women gathering around a coffee table has grown into a community‑led movement supporting over 400–500 women across Victoria, with growth underway beyond.

We exist to uplift, empower and equip women to rediscover confidence, purpose and potential — often described as “the gold mine within.”
