Get Involved

There are many ways to connect with THEE Coffee Table

THEE Coffee Table is built on connection, compassion and community. Whether you’re looking to participate, volunteer, partner or support our work, we welcome you.

Financial support, in‑kind contributions and sponsorships help ensure our programs remain accessible and sustainable.
